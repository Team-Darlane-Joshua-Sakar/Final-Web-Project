Challenges

Coding Competition

Join the coding competition! Open to all IT students interested in entering coding projects including: software, apps, programs, mobile prototypes, websites, games.
Coding experience determines levels: developing coders, capable coders and proficient coders. Perfect if you want to work in teams or individually.

Poster Competition

Join the poster competition, open to all IT students (VET, undergraduate and postgraduate).
Communicate an IT problem or solution visually, and demonstrate your skills to judges from industry and code fair visitors. Work in teams or individually.
