About

Background

The Charles Darwin University IT Code Fair is an annual event, designed to display talent from across the country and inspire those students that wish to take the next step in beginning their coding journey. The first IT Code Fair was held in 2014 and has been a successful event, providing excellent student oppurtunities every year since.





The fair encompasses a variety of activities, with students demonstrating and sharing their IT skills and enabling for the establishment of connections between students and industry professionals.
