Employee Speed Dating

Industry Employer Speed Dating provides graduating students with an opportunity to meet local employers to kick-start their career.
Up to 20 places are available. Preference is given to students in the final year of their degrees and to students who complete the preparation requirements below combine narrative with digital content, (including images, sound, and text).

Note: Participation in one of the Competitions/Challenges is compulsory to participate in Employer Speed Dating.

Preparation: To be eligible to be part of the event, students need to undertake the following tasks:
1. Attend a preparation workshop

2. Bring with you the completed first draft documents:
  • A completed Brief Bio (first draft - digital only) that will be sent to the employers BEFORE the Code Fair. You need to decide whether you want to be part of the Developer or IT support/ networking Speed Dating.
  • A copy of your resume (1 page - preferably paper) for review.
  • A draft of the ‘Pitch’ script.

On the Day:
Professional attire or code fair Tshirts is acceptable.You will have 3 minutes meet each industry.
